Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the website collects, uses, and protects your personal information. It also details your rights and options regarding your information.

Information Collection:

The website collects personal information like name, email address, mailing address, and phone number when you register, order, subscribe, respond to surveys, or enter information on the website.

Information Usage:

The website uses your information to personalize your experience, improve the website, process orders, respond to inquiries, run marketing campaigns, and comply with legal requirements.

Information Protection:

The website implements security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. They use secure networks, encryption, and regular malware scanning.

Cookies:

The website does not use cookies for tracking purposes but may use them for session management or to improve your experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

Third-party Disclosure:

The website does not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. They may share non-personally identifiable information for marketing or advertising purposes.

Third-party Links:

The website may contain links to third-party websites with separate privacy policies. The website is not responsible for the content or practices of these linked sites.

Google Ads:

The website uses Google Ads and may collect data about your activity on the website to show you targeted ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising using Google's settings.

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA):

The website complies with CalOPPA and allows users to visit the site anonymously and opt out of information sharing.

Do Not Track:

The website respects Do Not Track signals and does not track users or use advertising when a Do Not Track signal is detected.

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

The website does not collect information from children under 13 and does not allow third-party tracking of children's information.

Fair Information Practices:

The website follows Fair Information Practices and will notify you of a data breach within 7 business days.

CAN-SPAM Act:

The website complies with the CAN-SPAM Act and allows users to unsubscribe from receiving emails.
